Brief Bio:

I am currently a Professor at School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanjing University of Science and Technology. From 2018 to 2020, I was Research Scientist in Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI), Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systerm from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2016. From 2016 to 2018, I was a Postdoctoral Fellow in BRIC and IDEA lab, University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill (UNC-CH), USA.



Research Interests:

  • Medical image analysis;
  • Multi-view/modality learning;
  • Transfer learning;
  • Deep learning and applications;
  • Object detection/tracking.
